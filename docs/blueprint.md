# **App Name**: CITU Connect

## Core Features:

- Homepage Carousel: Display a carousel of relevant images on the homepage, auto-sliding every 5 seconds.
- Information Cards: Display key information categories (Holidays list, Promotions, Images, RH/PER QR codes, Pay chart, incentive rate, NRH OP timing) as interactive cards.
- Navigation to Sub-Pages: Each information card links to a separate page with specific content for that category.
- Social Media Integration: Include Instagram and WhatsApp icons in the footer, linked to the respective channels.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep red (#8B0000) to represent the union's strength and passion, balanced with elegance.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F0F0) for a clean, professional feel, ensuring readability against the red and black elements.
- Accent color: Dark gray (#333333) to provide contrast and a touch of sophistication in text and borders.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans' for a modern yet approachable style, suitable for both headlines and body text.
- Use clean and simple icons, primarily in red or dark gray, to represent each information category.
- Maintain a balanced and symmetrical layout to enhance the professional appearance. Prioritize readability.
- Subtle fade-in animations on image carousel transitions.
